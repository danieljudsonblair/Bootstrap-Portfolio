About Me

One day I eclipsed the eclipse. Does that make me an astronomer? An astrophysicist? An astrologer? Because I have a fortune for you.

My experience could be described as disjointed. I (sort of) adopted a rooster and blogged about it during the MySpace era. I ponder reactionless propulsion on a regular basis. I have prepared and cooked over 1 million pizzas. I love playing music, as in guitar, drums, and bass. I have been trying for 15+ years to create a consistently profitable automated futures trading algorithm. I enjoy moderate to strong turbulence on airplanes; that is, until everyone else starts throwing up :/

I feel like I just want what most other people want in life: to find their place and thrive.
